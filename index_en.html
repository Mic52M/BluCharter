A Unique Experience on Monte Argentario

Experience an unforgettable day aboard our exclusive boat: a unique service to discover the wonders of Monte Argentario's sea, among hidden coves, crystal clear waters, and breathtaking views. From morning to sunset, let yourself be guided by the passion for the sea and the comfort of an experience designed just for you and your travel companions.

🛥️

Day on the Boat

Daily Schedule
9:15 AM - 5:45/6:00 PM

Departure from Porto Ercole harbor and navigation along the Monte Argentario coast: swimming, relaxation, sun, and nature in an exclusive setting.

🛏️
Weekend on board (Friday-Sunday or Saturday-Sunday) with overnight stay included!
The boat is equipped with air conditioning and offers 3 cabins: one double, two twins with single beds, and a convertible dinette, for a total of 7 berths.
🍽️

Seafood Lunch on Board

During the day

Enjoy a lunch with fresh seafood pasta prepared on board and, in the afternoon, savor a delicious ice cream while admiring the sea: a moment of taste and conviviality immersed in the beauty of Monte Argentario.

🥪
Bring your own lunch!
If you prefer, you can bring your own meal on board without using the kitchen. Enjoy your day in total freedom, savoring your favorite food surrounded by the sea and nature, with no time or menu constraints.
🍹

Sunset Aperitif

6:30 PM until 8:30 PM... or maybe even 9 PM, if the sunset is special!

End your day with an exclusive aperitif on the boat: selected drinks, appetizers, and the sun diving into Monte Argentario's sea.

⏰
You can also book just the aperitif!
On selected days, you can book only the sunset aperitif: departure from the harbor at 6:30 PM and return after sunset, to experience the magic of the sun setting over the sea in a short but intense experience.
